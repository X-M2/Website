About

Who's keeping this notebook

MindMatters started as a Final Year Assignment by a team of polytechnic students who noticed the same thing in themselves and their classmates: stress rarely announces itself until it's already loud. This notebook is our attempt at catching it earlier — small, honest, and easy to reach for before the weather turns.

MindMatters — a student project, not a medical service.
